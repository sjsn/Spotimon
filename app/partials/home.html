Instructions

    • The Game

    • Spotimon is a mixture of trivia and Pokemon! Battle Spotify tracks in order to catch them.
    • In battle, you can either Attack an opponent or Run from them. Attacking allows you to weaken them by stating facts you know about the track such as an artist that produced it or the title of the album it was released with. Be careful! You only get 5 Attack attempts total! If you don't know anything about the track then you can Run. This action will take you to the next artist.
    • There are a total of 20 Spotimon per game. When you Run from a track, you will never see it again.
    • Once caught, a track lives inside your Journal. Access your Journal at any time to see which tracks you've caught and which ones you've missed!
